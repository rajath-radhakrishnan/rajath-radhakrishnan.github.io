Workshops & Conferences

Diving Deeper into Defects: On the Intersection of Field Theory, Quantum Matter, and Mathematics

Along with Adam Chalabi (NBI), Vincentas Mulevicius (Uni Vienna) and Philine van Vliet (ENS Paris) I am organizing a workshop at the Isaac Newton Institute for Mathematical Sciences to discuss recent results and explore open problems at the intersection of quantum field theory, quantum matter and mathematics. This workshop is part of the four-month programme Quantum Field Theory with Boundaries, Impurities, and Defects.
