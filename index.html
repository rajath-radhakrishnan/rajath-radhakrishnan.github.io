About

I am a postdoctoral fellow at The Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Trieste, Italy. Before joining ICTP, I completed my MSc at Imperial College London and PhD at Queen Mary University of London. My research focuses on exploring generalized symmetries of quantum field theories and their interplay with higher-category theory and quantum information theory.
