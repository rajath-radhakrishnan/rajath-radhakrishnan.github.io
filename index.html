About

I am a postdoctoral research associate at the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford. Before joining Oxford, I was a postdoctoral fellow at The Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics. My research focuses on exploring generalized symmetries of quantum field theories and their interplay with higher-category theory and quantum information theory.

My CV can be found here. My publications in high-energy physics can be found here. My full list of publications including some older papers on quantum information theory can be found here.
